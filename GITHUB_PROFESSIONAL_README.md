## 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project
designed to analyze global sales performance across regions, countries,
product categories, and sales channels.

This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful visual
insights that support strategic and data-driven decision-making.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Objectives

-   Analyze regional and country-level sales performance\
-   Compare Online vs Offline sales channels\
-   Identify most profitable product categories\
-   Track revenue trends over time\
-   Understand correlation between revenue, profit, and units sold

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset includes:

-   Region\
-   Country\
-   Item Type\
-   Sales Channel (Online / Offline)\
-   Order Date\
-   Units Sold\
-   Unit Cost\
-   Total Revenue\
-   Total Cost\
-   Total Profit

### 📌 Calculated Metrics

-   **Total Profit = Total Revenue -- Total Cost**
-   Aggregated measures using SUM and statistical analysis

# 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

## 🔹 Distribution Analysis

-   Violin Plot -- Profit distribution by Sales Channel\
-   Correlation Heatmap -- Revenue, Profit & Units Sold

## 🔹 Regional & Product Analysis

-   Stacked Area Chart -- Profit, Unit Cost & Units Sold\
-   Funnel Chart -- Profit by Item Type\
-   Area Chart -- Profit & Units by Region

## 🔹 Geographic Analysis

-   Bubble Map -- Revenue & Cost by Country\
-   Filled Map -- Units Sold & Profit by Region

## 🔹 Performance Monitoring

-   KPI Cards -- Revenue, Profit, Units Sold\
-   Gauge Chart -- Revenue Target Achievement\
-   Donut & Pie Charts -- Revenue Distribution\
-   Network Graph -- Country & Sales Channel Relationships\
-   Line Chart -- Revenue Trend Over Time\
-   Scatter Chart -- Cost vs Units Sold
